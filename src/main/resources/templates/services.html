Services

By now we understand a bit about how applications are being managed in TAS, what about services? Let's think of services as external application dependencies like a datastore or messaging system. But it can represent many other things that we would not typically think of it.

Remember the application diagram from the main page?

We often hear about The Twelve-Factor App as a great methodology for building modern applications. The Tanzu Application Service Service strategy is very much like IV - Backing Services factor. Services are attached, in TAS' case, bound to applications.
